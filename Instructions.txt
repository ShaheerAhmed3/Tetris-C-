1. Do not press escape during the game, it will terminate the program.
2. Use the arrows keys as controls.
3. Up key to rotate.
4. Down, Left and Right key to move down, left and right respectively.
5. The images whose names are colors are the pngs for the blocks, rest of the images are for backgrounds.
6. Only one font is used in the code.
7. Leaderboard will be displayed in the end.
8. Enjoy!
